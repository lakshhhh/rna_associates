About us

What we do

NHBS LLP is a business consultancy and innovative services organisation, established to help bring clarity to clients’ environment, be it inventory, fixed assets, (for the benefit of owners, lenders or operations), accounting or an assessment of processes being followed. We provide services in business consulting and help companies set up shared services to improve operating efficiencies and achieve business and operational excellence. Housed in Mumbai, NHBS LLP has over 500+ professionals and a pan India network in over 200 cities. We believe in the opportunity to serve and improve the performance of enterprises, and the ecosystem(s) that they operate in. Therefore, our client portfolio ranges from large MNC’s to small firms across industries and geographical space. Our delivery is unmatched in value creation, enabled by a platform of PAN-India presence, led by talented and experienced professionals. NHBS LLP brings superior resources and capabilities to our clients like never before in India. Globally we work with the executives, through NHBS LLP-Global, to help them make better decisions, convert those decisions to actions and deliver the sustainable success they desire.
